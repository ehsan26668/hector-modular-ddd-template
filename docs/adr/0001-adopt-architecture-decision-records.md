# ADR-0001: Adopt Architecture Decision Records (ADR)

## Status

Accepted

## Context

To ensure long-term maintainability and traceability of architectural decisions in this template, we need a formal mechanism to document decisions, their rationale, and consequences.

## Decision

We will use Architecture Decision Records (ADR) for all significant design and architectural decisions.

## Consequences

- Every major decision will be documented in `docs/adr/`.
- Decisions will be trackable and reviewable.
- Future developers will understand the "why" behind existing architecture.
